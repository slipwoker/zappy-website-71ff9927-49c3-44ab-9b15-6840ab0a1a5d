Good to know

We've gathered the essentials to help you plan your stay with clarity and ease.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in begins at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early arrivals and late departures can be arranged based on availability—please contact us in advance.

Quiet Hours

To ensure all guests enjoy a restful experience, we kindly ask that noise levels be kept low between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM throughout the resort grounds.

Parking & Arrival

Complimentary parking is available for all guests. Upon arrival, our team will assist with luggage and provide directions to your accommodation.

Accessibility

Santa Casa Resort is committed to providing accessible accommodations. Please let us know your specific needs when booking so we can ensure your comfort.

Pet Policy

We welcome well-behaved pets in select rooms with advance notice. A small additional fee applies. Please contact us to confirm availability and requirements.

Payment & Cancellation

For detailed information about payment methods, deposit requirements, and our cancellation policy, please reach out to our reservations team directly.
